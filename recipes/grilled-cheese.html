Grilled Cheese

The Bestest Sandwich

Grilled cheese is a classic American sandwich that has been around since 1920. It is a hot sandwich made with buttered and toasted bread and originally filled with American cheese, but is now commonly made with one or more different cheeses.

Ingredients

1 serving

  • 2 slices Bread
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Slices Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread 1/2 Tbsp butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place a skillet over low to medium/low heat. A griddle should be at 275ËšF. Immediately add 2 slices of bread with the butter-side-down.
  3. Stack cheeses on one of the pieces of bread, cover with the other piece of bread and flip the sandwich over.
  4. Continue cooking until both side are golden
