Spam, Eggs, and Rice

A Delicious Pacific Island Meal

SPAM eggs and rice is a simple yet popular dish in Hawaii. While traditionally served for breakfast, it's also great for lunch, dinner, or even a snack!

Ingredients

2 servings

  • 2 cups Rice
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 can SPAM

Steps

  1. Start your rice cooking first. If you don't know how to then you're gonna need another guide.
  2. Cut your slices of SPAM into 1/2 inch cubes.
  3. When rice has about 10 minutes to go, whisk your eggs.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet on medium high and spray with cooking spray. Pour the eggs in. Scramble 'em.
  5. Cook the SPAM in the same skillet until a golden brown crust forms on the cubes.
  6. Scoop rice onto a plate and top with eggs and SPAM
